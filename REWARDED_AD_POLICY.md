# REWARDED_AD_POLICY

## GENERAL_RULES

- Ads must be optional.
- Core gameplay must function without watching ads.
- Reward must provide immediate gameplay benefit.
- Never force ads to continue playing.
- Avoid aggressive popups.

## ALLOWED_REWARD_TYPES

1. Reward Double
   - Example: Double gold after stage clear.

2. Revive
   - Example: Continue after game over.

3. Special Chest
   - Example: Open rare reward chest.

4. Random Buff Event
   - Example: Temporary power boost.

## FREQUENCY_LIMITS

- Max 2 ad opportunities per session.
- Never chain ads.
- Do not show ads back-to-back.
- Suggest ad only once per core loop.

## BALANCE_RULES

- Ads must not break progression.
- Game must remain playable without ads.
- Rewards should accelerate progress, not replace gameplay.

## ADDITIONAL_RULES

- Show a clear "No thanks" option on every rewarded ad prompt.
- Add at least one gameplay loop cooldown between ad opportunities.
- Do not offer rewarded ads during critical tutorial steps.
- Clearly state the exact reward before the ad starts.
- If ad loading fails, return users to gameplay immediately without penalty.
