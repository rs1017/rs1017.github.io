# MOBILE_PRODUCT_POLICY

## PLATFORM

- Target platform: Android only.
- No iOS support at this stage.
- Use Android App Bundle (AAB) for release.
- Keep build pipeline simple.

## MONETIZATION_MODEL

- Monetization method: Rewarded Ads only.
- No banner ads.
- No forced interstitial ads.
- No in-app purchases at initial stage.

## DEVELOPMENT_STRATEGY

- Solo indie development.
- No paid user acquisition.
- Natural discovery only.
- Development time per product under 60 days.
- Keep maintenance cost minimal.

## PRODUCT_PHILOSOPHY

- Every repository is a product.
- No experimental-only mindset.
- Ship fast.
- Avoid over-engineering.
